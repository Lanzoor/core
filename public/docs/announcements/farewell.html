a farewell letter

Hello everyone.
Recently, I've been struggling to find a reason to continue living.

I wish this was some sort of an ARG, a puzzle, or an April Fools prank.
I also wish that this was another one of those "im going to kill myself" "jk" statements I've made before.
I genuinely wish. But it isn't.

This document tries to list all of the issues in much detail as possible.
Please expect writing to be poor and rushed. I did not have enough time to write this, unlike the other projects I've worked on.
And please, don't try and force yourself into reading the entire thing.

[ 01 ] Nothing is truly fun anymore.

Motivation Issues

Seriously, I used to enjoy programming stuff, listening to music, learning new languages, playing games and watching anime. However, after a certain turning point, I've constantly been dealing with intense motivation issues. My thoughts often become scattered or shut down entirely, and things that mattered to me feel distant, boring, and completely impossible to engage with.

Making Progress

To be honest, I don't feel like I'm achieving anything in life anymore. School has been draining my energy and the pressure is so intense. I completely dropped out of all cram schools because of my mental issues. I know, cram schools are absolutely necessary in order to literally survive in the current state of South Korea. My life is completely doomed, and there's no point in trying to improve it.

[ 02 ] I just hate my life.

Medical Background

I've had a rather intense medical background, and they still affect my thoughts to this day.

My life was a disaster ever since the beginning. When I was young, I was diagnosed with retinoblastoma (retina cancer) and ended up losing the sight completely in my right eye. Even to this day, I have to wear an artifical eye

But that wasn't the end. I was diagnosed with osteosarcoma (bone cancer) on my left leg when I was around 10. I had to spend around 7 months in a hospital, which forced me into both social and psychological isolation during a critical stage of my development. During that time, I had very limited opportunities to learn how to socialize naturally, and I later developed ADHD, OCD, social anxiety, and related difficulties.

I regret everything.

I regret running the social experiment in JPA. If that didn't happen, then I wouldn't have to spend a month of my life, write ~700 liness of html code in order to make an exposal, JUST to see it getting completely disrespected and ignored by the moderators.

I regret joining Discord. If that didn't happen, then I would've been living a better life. I wouldn't have to worry about maintaining friendships, or any of the drama happened before.

I regret being born in this family. If that didn't happen, then I wouldn't have suffered from intense psychological abuse. I wouldn't have been affected by genetic trait of being vulnerable to cancer. I wouldn't have suffered from all of that medical background.

I regret being born in this world, as well as EVERY choice I've ever made. If that didn't happen, then maybe I wouldn't be suffering so much right now.

In conclusion, I genuinely think it's better to end all of this, instead of forcing myself into regretting everything,

[ -1 ] Conclusion

Please understand that I am not sharing this in order to ask for help or empathy.
I've tried several approaches before to fix the issues above, including talking to friends, parents, and/or therapists; self-harm; taking a digital detox; and more.
None of these methods helped or resolved the problem.
At this point, I really feel like the best option is to put all of this into an end.

I don't want to say that I'm going to be killing myself.
It's not because I've found a method to overcome all of these issues, or I am afraid of doing such action.
It's because I genuinely can't find a way to kill myself in peace. Everytime I try to self-harm or suicide, something inside me tells me to stop no matter what I try. If you have any advice on self-harm or suicide, PLEASE message me. I'm begging.
