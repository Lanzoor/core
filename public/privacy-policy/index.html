Data Collection

This website collects a small amount of information about visits for analytics and troubleshooting purposes. The information collected is limited to the following:

  • The pathname of your visit. This is used to measure page views and understand which pages are visited most frequently.
  • The country associated with your visit. This is used to understand the geographic distribution of visitors and identify which regions are using the website.
  • The timestamp of your visit. This is used to determine when visits occurred and to assist with troubleshooting issues that may be related to specific website versions or deployments.
  • The referrer. This indicates which page or website led you to this website. It is used to understand how visitors discover the website and which external sources are directing traffic to it.
  • The user-agent of your visit. This is used to determine whether the request was authentic or not.

No advertising cookies are used, and no user accounts are required to access the website. The collected data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

This website's analytics system is open source. You may review the implementation in the source code.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like data associated with your visits to be removed, feel free to get in touch and provide as much information as possible about the visit, such as the approximate date, page visited, and country associated with the request. While deletion requests will be handled in good faith, it may not always be possible to identify a specific visit record.
