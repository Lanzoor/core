Change Prefixes

Every change is prefixed with a symbol indicating what happened.

  • A green plus sign (+) indicates that something has been added.
  • A yellow arrow (>) indicates that something has been changed or improved.
  • A red minus sign (-) indicates that something has been removed.
  • In some cases, a red ! may appear before the symbol. This means the change is significant, and changes major aspects of the website.
  • In some cases, a blue p may appear after the symbol. This means the change is provisional and may be reverted in the future.
  • Please note that prefixes are only intended as a quick summary of a change. They are subjective and may not fully describe everything included in an update. For example, an entry marked with a plus sign may also contain modifications to existing features.
