How to Contribute

First of all, please don't feel pressured to contribute anything to this website.
It's meant to be open and welcoming for anyone to visit, and your presence alone already means a lot.
That saiddd, (insert dramatic pause here) there are a few different ways you can support the site, all of which I value equally.

Sharing Your Thoughts

If you have any suggestions, concerns, ideas, or issues, getting in touch is a wonderful way to contribute.
Feedback helps me improve and motivates me to keep building new features.

Joining Development

If you'd like to get hands-on, you can help by opening a pull request.
I'll carefully review changes, and contributors may be recognized in the credits section as a way of saying thanks.

Please remember: this way of contributing should never be used with malice. For details, see the contribution guidelines.

Simply Being Here

Visiting and exploring the site itself is a very meaningful contribution.
Your interest keeps the project alive, and I truly appreciate you spending time here.
I update the site with passion almost every day, so you'll see it evolve over time.
Even though hosting has costs, the joy of creating and sharing easily outweighs them.
So please drop by whenever you like, and most importantly: have a great day! 👋
